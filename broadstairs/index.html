What is a Hackerspace?

A hackerspace is for creative people who are passionate about technology.

We live in a science and technology based society. This knowledge that makes society wealthy and prosperous is open for all, should not be the preserve of a few. Hakerspaces are a way to facilitate the diffusion of this knowledge-vitality throughout the body of general society.

By providing an open space 24x7, people ebb and flow with a strong DIY attitude and self-help ethic. A hackerspace is a location where people with common interests, often in computers, technology, science, digital or electronic art can meet, socialise and/or collaborate. Hackerspaces are open to all types and all interests, and aim to be inclusive.

They are a new phenomena in the last few years which have spread quickly around the world.
